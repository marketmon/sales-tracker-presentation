9th Street.

Tran moved to Philadelphia after the Vietnam War and has operated her produce stand ever since. Her employees serve customers in Spanish and English. She runs everything in Vietnamese.

The problem, in action
Every. Single. Sale.
The manual process.
  • 1 Customer hands over a basket of produce
  • 2 Adds up prices in her head
  • 3 Punches individual prices into a calculator
  • 4 Bags items, hands them back

No record. No data. No way to know what sold best last Tuesday.

What if tracking sales
was completely
hands-free?
The Solution
Speak an order.
We handle the rest.
  • ✓ Transcribes speech on-device — no cloud API
  • ✓ Vietnamese, Spanish, and English
  • ✓ Parses items and maps to catalog prices
  • ✓ Vendor confirms before anything is logged
  • ✓ Dashboard: top items, revenue over time
Step 1 — Record

Select a language, tap the mic, speak the order naturally.

Step 2 — Confirm

Review line items, adjust quantities, add anything missed — then confirm.

Dashboard

Every transaction logged. Top items, revenue over time, full paginated history.
